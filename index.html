The Maker

Craft rooted in
precision & vision.

Likhâ — from the Filipino word meaning "to create" — was born from a belief that tiling is far more than a trade. It is a discipline, a language of texture, and when placed with intention, an art form.

As a BCA-certified tile artisan with training in interior design and digital illustration, I bridge structural precision with artistic sensibility — designing installations that serve a space while elevating it entirely.

Every commission begins with listening: to the space, to the light, to the life that will unfold within it. What emerges is not just a tiled surface — it is a considered composition.

Design Language

Signature
Collections.

Karst
Freestyle Handpainted · Geometric & Abstract
Each tile painted entirely freehand — no guides, no repeats. Bold geometric compositions and fluid abstract mark-making create installations that feel less like tiling and more like hanging a painting. Raw, confident, completely original.
Karst FaultKarst Obsidian Karst FractureKarst RidgeKarst Cavern
Monolith
Minimalist · Precision-Laid
Large-format tiles in a single dominant tone, laid with architectural precision. Restraint as a design philosophy — the craft is in the placement, not the pattern.
Monolith SlateMonolith Ash Monolith VoidMonolith Plane
Archipelago
Waterslide & Ceramic Decals
Delicate, detailed imagery transferred onto tile with extraordinary precision — coastal, botanical, and heritage motifs rendered in porcelain-like quality.
Archipelago TideArchipelago Drift Archipelago ShoreArchipelago Pearl
Flora
Embossed from Real Plants & Flowers
Living plants and flowers pressed directly into tile before firing, leaving a permanent tactile impression of nature in ceramic. Every piece carries the exact imprint of a real plant — genuinely one of a kind. Each sub-name honours a woman who shaped the maker.
Flora LornaFlora Vilma Flora CarolinaFlora Violeta Flora ElsaFlora Gina Flora Teresa
Relievo
Handpainted Using Stencils
Precision meets artistry. Each tile hand-painted through carefully crafted stencils — crisp, repeating patterns with the warmth and slight variation that only a human hand can give.
Relievo CrestRelievo Lattice Relievo ArcadeRelievo Crown
Meridian
Patterned Stamps
Rhythmic, tactile, deeply rooted in craft tradition. Each tile hand-stamped with carved pattern blocks — warm, slightly imperfect repeat designs that carry the character of the maker's hand.
Meridian SpiceMeridian Ember Meridian SiennaMeridian Harvest
