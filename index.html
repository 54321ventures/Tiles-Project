The Belief

— The Likhâ Principle

The Maker

Craft as
calling.

Likhâ — from the Filipino word meaning "to create" — was born from the belief that tiling is far more than a trade. As a BCA-certified tile artisan with training in interior design and digital illustration, I bridge structural precision with artistic vision.

Every commission begins with listening: to the space, to the light, to the life that will unfold within it. What emerges is not just a tiled surface — it is a considered composition.
